# LinkedIn Progress Post

I am building an in-progress PostgreSQL DBA home lab to practice real database administration concepts in a hands-on way.

So far, I have set up a PostgreSQL lab environment, connected with pgAdmin, created a lab database, organized schemas, designed relational tables, documented primary and foreign keys, added constraints and indexes, exported an ERD, and collected supporting evidence through screenshots and structured documentation.

I am also starting backup and recovery planning as part of the lab. My next steps are to add sample data, practice backup and restore, expand reporting views, review permissions and roles, and work on monitoring/log review.

The goal is not to present this as a production system. The goal is to build practical DBA skills, document the work clearly, and keep improving the lab one milestone at a time.
